A unique merchant bank

Ash Creek is a boutique Merchant Bank dedicated to assisting businesses who have a clear vision of their goals and objectives.
Over 20+ years and 30+ investments, Ash Creek members have assisted small and mid-market companies to accelerate revenue growth and, only then, provide investors with a meaningful return.

Business development

Especially for startups, most entrepreneurs have a keen eye for market needs, product viability and have built a workable technology, product, or solution. But not all of them have a competitive advantage in distribution.
And that makes all the difference.
Where needed, Ash Creek members roll up their sleeves to make warm introductions and/or to follow through to enterprise sales.

Investments

Most of our investments have been made in conjunction with family offices and other investment groups. It is not unusual for us to create a Special Purpose Entity (SPE), a limited liability company, for investing in situations where the investment may be larger. There is no minimum investment.
Before we invest, we conduct extensive due diligence to make sure the economic, marketing and financial models all work together.
Finally, one of the businesses funded back in 2019 has become a Unicorn and will exit the year with a $1 billion valuation.
